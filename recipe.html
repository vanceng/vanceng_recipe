Super Green Juice Recipe

Green up your Morning.

Check out this quick 5 minute recipe to jumpstart your morning!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup filtered water
  • 3 small cucumbers, chopped
  • 1 1/2 celery stalks, quartered
  • 3 large apples, seeded and quartered
  • 2 cups loosely packed kale leaves
  • 1 cup loosely packed spinach leaves
  • 4 large lemons, seeded and quartered

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender in the order listed and secure lid.
  2. Turn the machine on and slowly increase the speed to High. Blend for 30-45 seconds.
  3. Strain through a nut milk bag. Store in refrigerator. Enjoy within 2 days.
